    About

    This project approaches the two sides of the investment on real estate. On one side we have the investor that wants to place his properties to rent; in the other hand, we have the client that is looking for a property to rent and consequently inhabit.

    In an attempt to support both sides, we focus on develop a simple and intuitive website. We provide the following functionalities:

  • Insert a property;
  • Request a property;
  • Book a property;
  • Remove a property;
  • Consult a property.

    FAQ

  • How can I turn my house available for rent?

    • In order to do this, you have to be registered and logged in. Then, go to your profile and check the New House section and follow the current steps.

  • Who provides the house's rate?

    • The clients who already rented a specific house, after delivering it, they can rate the house out of 10.

  • How can I get in touch with the website's owner?

    • Check the Team section and look for our e-mails.
